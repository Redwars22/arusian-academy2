# ALPT Grammar Corpus

In this page you will find all the grammar topics covered by ALPT exam organized by
the Arusian government and they are ordered by level, with ALPT E being the lowest (equivalent
to A1 in the European language level reference) being the lowest and ALPT A being the highest
(equivalent to C1 and C2 levels in the forementioned guide). Each grammar topic will be followed 
by examples and grammatical explanations for beginners. Whenever possible, we will also provide advanced
linguistic descriptions for sentences for more advanced students and Linguistics enthusiasts.
